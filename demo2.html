The library has 3 levels:

  • Level 1: contains highschool math courses.
  • Level 2: contains college and university math courses.
  • Level 3: contains programming, web development and computer science courses.

Important note

Some courses contain specific statements that are designed to end on the same line where they start even if they don't fully fit the screen so that their content is not split into multiple lines. This is done for better visibility and clarity.

On mobile devices, you will be able to scroll horizontally such statements to view the hidden parts, while on computers, a scrollbar will appear, allowing you to scroll horizontally using that.

There will be an exclamation mark (!) at the beginning of such statements to indicate this feature, however, if the entire statement is already visible on your screen, scrolling will not work, even if the exclamation mark is present.

In courses where this feature is used, we'll leave a reminder at the beginning of the page.
